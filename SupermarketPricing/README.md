# SupermarketPricing

Designing system to manage pricing goods at supermarkets. In this demo we are covering below points.

1. managing complax price of goods 

   Ex:
		I.  three for a dollar (so what’s the price if I buy 4, or 5?)
		II. $1.99/pound (so what does 4 ounces cost?)
		III. buy two, get one free (so does the third item have a price?)

2. Stock management.
3. manage pricing schemes.
4.manage cost and prices.
